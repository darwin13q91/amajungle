**Where:** RDO 81 - Argao (covers Dalaguete)  
**Address:** BIR RDO No. 81, Argao, Cebu

**Steps:**
- [ ] **Step 1:** Get TIN (if don't have one)
  - BIR Form 1901 (for self-employed/business)
  - Valid IDs
  - DTI Certificate
  - Cedula

- [ ] **Step 2:** Register Business
  - BIR Form 1901 (Application for Registration)
  - DTI Certificate
  - Barangay Permit
  - Mayor's Permit
  - Valid IDs

- [ ] **Step 3:** Pay Registration Fee (~‚Ç±500)

- [ ] **Step 4:** Register Books of Accounts
  - Purchase accounting books
  - Have them registered/stamped at BIR

- [ ] **Step 5:** Get Authority to Print (ATP)
  - BIR Form 1906
  - Choose Official Receipts/Invoices format
  - Get ATP from BIR

- [ ] **Step 6:** Print Official Receipts/Invoices
  - Find BIR-accredited printer
  - Print OR/SI with ATP
